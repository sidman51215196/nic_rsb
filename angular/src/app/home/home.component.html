Welcome to Rajya Sainik Board

The Rajya Sainik Board, Delhi has been established to promote welfare and resettlement of ex-servicemen, war widows disabled ex-servicemen and families of defence personnel belonging to the State.
Processing Second World War Veteran Pension applications, issuing ex- servicemen Identity Cards, rendering employment assistance to ex-servicemen,war widows/disabled ex-servicemen and to the members of the families of deceased Defence Service personnel are the main functions of these Boards.
